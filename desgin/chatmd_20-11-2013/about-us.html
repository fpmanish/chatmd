About Us
Willandra Medical Clinic incorporating Northern Industrial Clinic has been involved in industrial medicine since 1983. As well as being a general practice we are also equipped to provide numerous services that are required by employers.

This exceptional facility offers patients a complete range of medical services in the one convenient location. As well as providing patients with the assurance of the latest technology in diagnostic and treatment facilities, the range of services on site will reduce time required to be away from work and family.
