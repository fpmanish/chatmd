Is there a term for a quote that comes after a sub heading?
A.
Sometimes a quote is just a qoute, but ifthe content is ismilar to that of a book epigraph or chapter epigraph ( perhaps including a source line)—that is, something pithy or funny thoughtful, rather than a run-of-the-mill chunk of supporting material—then call it an epigraph or section epigraph.
